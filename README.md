## 3. Compare and contrast Agile and Waterfall methodologies. What are the main advantages and disadvantages of each?
1. Agile -Uses  Iterative and incremental approach to project
management and software development WHILE WATERFALL uses Linear and sequential approach.
2. Advantages and Disadvantages of Agile
   a) Advantages
   1. Quick response to changes.
   2. Regular customer feedback.
      b)Disadvantages
   1. Less predictability.
   2. Requires significant customer involvement.
  
3.  Advantages and Disadvantages of Waterfall
   1. Clear structure and documentation.
   2. Easy to manage due to its rigidity.
2. Disadvantages:
  1.  Difficult to accommodate changes.
  2. Testing occurs late in the process.
## 4. In what types of projects might Agile be more beneficial than Waterfall, and vice versa? Can you provide examples of each? 
Agile is more beneficial for projects with evolving requirements for example mobile app development where user feedback is needed WHILE Waterfall is more beneficial for projects with stable requirements for example government contracts or projects requiring regulatory compliance.
## 5. What are some methods for ensuring quality assurance throughout a software project? Why is it important to maintain high standards? 
Code reviews, walkthroughs, Automated testing and manual inspections. 
Maintaining high standards ensures reliability, enhances user satisfaction, and reduces long-term maintenance costs.


## 6. How does defining the project scope contribute to successful project planning? What is a Work Breakdown Structure (WBS), and why is it useful?
BY setting clear expectations and boundaries, which helps in resource allocation and prioritizing tasks.
2. Work Breakdown Structure (WBS):
A WBS is a hierarchical decomposition of the project into smaller, manageable components. It’s useful for:
 Facilitating task assignment.
 Estimating costs and durations.
 Enhancing project visibility.
## 7. What are the benefits of developing a detailed project schedule, and how can Gantt charts assist in this process? 
A detailed project schedule helps manage time effectively, allocate resources, and track progress WHILE Gantt charts visually represent project timelines, showing task durations, dependencies, and milestones, which aids in clear communication and scheduling.
